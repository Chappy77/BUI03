How Do We Do It?

We focus on Edge Acceleration and Edge API Traffic Shaping—API/REST calls at layer 7—and look at every request coming in at the edge where we can apply distributed business rules. For example, we can slow down new users so that existing users can check-out. In addition, we improve TCP, network and user monitoring parameters to make your APIs fast.

It's what we do at the edge.

We intercept the traffic—via DNS setting, at the edge, away from your data center. Early ACK / SSL termination, bus rules at the edge, plus a dozen more TCP, network and user monitoring advancements keep your data center safe. You get both traffic shaping and lower latency.

So...what else?

Frequent requests include screen scrapes and dictionary attack prevention. By doing a bit more sophisticated local edge rate limiting, we can prevent these and DDoS attacks. Nothing to change in your mobile app or data center. Everything is via our dashboard.
