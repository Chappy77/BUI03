So... how does it work?

We intercept the traffic—via DNS setting. Nothing to change in your app or data center. Everything is via our dashboard. Plus we are working to bring on other API DDNs so you have a choice of providers.

Early SSL termination.

Early ACK. Apply bus rules at edge. Plus a dozen more TCP, Network and user monitoring settings. If you do each, the aggregate will give you both traffic shaping and lower latency.

You can shut off any new requests, allow only shopping carts in progress, turn off traffic by regions until traffic normalizes. Ask us to show you.
