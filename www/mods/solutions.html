So... how does it work?

As soon as our proprietary proxy intercepts the API call - we improve the BGP plan and such. Some ISPs, surprisingly to some, route the traffic the long way to your data center.

And what else

Early SSL termination. Early ACK. Apply bus rules at edge. Plus a dozen more TCP, Network and user monitoring settings. If you do each, the aggregate will give you both traffic shaping and lower latency.
