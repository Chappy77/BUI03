    • API Performance at the Edge

    We look at every request coming in at the edge. This is where we speed up traffic and prevent DDoS and other attacks keeping your business up and running at high performance levels even under attack. How can you get attacked if the attackers don't know your data center IPs?






    Edge Traffic Shaping

    Apakau is transparently enabled by adding a CName in your DNS settings to Apakau Geo-DNS. No modifications to the API, application, or data center are required. We optimize all REST/mobile API calls to make your APIs and mobile application fast.


    Edge Business Rules

    Our ability to implement business rules at the edge keeps your data center safe and app running smoothly. Early ACK and SSL termination keeps attackers away from your data center. No more DDoS, page scraping, dictionary attacks and more.


    See How It Works

    Set a sample business rule on our dashboard: For example, at 70% endpoint load, do not let new requests other than those in checkout mode to the endpoint. At 80%, shut off all requests by country, and at 90%, allow traffic from only one country.
